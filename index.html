From the Principal's Desk

There can never be Academic Excellence without discipline and as well can never be discipline without academic excellence. That’s why St. Anthony’s Catholic Comprehensive Institute (SACCI) is a centre of excellence, where education serves as a tool for the total development of the students.

Excellence is a journey and Discipline is the vehicle. With discipline, we inculcate good morals and skills in students for self-reliance and to meet the standard for all professional courses in tertiary institutions.

Discipline is thus the bridge between goals and accomplishment. In academics, sports, culture and arts, we set the pace and mount as champions in all competitions. Above all, our academic environment endowed with a beautiful landscape, decorated with trees and beautiful flowers adds extra colours to the overall formation of our students.
Oh! What a citadel of learning.

Our Mission

To provide education, life skills and moral values in a safe and supportive environment for holistic empowerment and development of the child.

Our Vision

SACCIA seeks to create a generation of highly motivated and skilled youths equipped to meet the challenges of high education, work & life through the development of supportive learning environment that encourages high moral discipline and academic excellence.
